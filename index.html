Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India • People’s President • Visionary Scientist

(1931 – 2015)

Famous Quote

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts, and thoughts result in action.”

Biography

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a vital role in India’s civilian space program and military missile development, earning the title “Missile Man of India”.

Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he rose from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected leaders of the nation. His life was a true example of dedication, simplicity, and excellence.

Dr. Kalam was deeply loved by students across the country. Through his speeches and books like Wings of Fire, he motivated millions to dream big and serve the nation.
